What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity refers to the practices and technologies designed to protect systems, networks, and data from digital attacks, theft, and damage.

Key Areas in Cybersecurity

  • Network Security: Protects internal networks from threats by securing routers, firewalls, and communication channels.
  • Application Security: Protects software and apps from vulnerabilities during development and deployment.
  • Information Security: Protects data integrity and privacy from unauthorized access.

What is Ethical Hacking?

Ethical Hacking is the practice of legally breaking into systems to identify security weaknesses and fix them before malicious hackers can exploit them.

Core Topics in Hacking & Cybersecurity

  • Penetration Testing: Simulated cyberattacks to test systems' security defenses.
  • Cryptography: Techniques to encrypt and decrypt data to ensure secure communication.
  • Network Security: Firewalls, IDS/IPS, VPNs, and secure protocols to protect data transmission.
