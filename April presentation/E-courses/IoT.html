Internet of Things (IoT)

Connecting the digital world with the physical

What is IoT?

Internet of Things (IoT) refers to a network of interconnected physical devices that communicate and exchange data using the internet.

Key Concepts

  • Embedded Systems: Dedicated computer systems built into devices to control functions.
  • IoT Security: Protecting IoT devices and networks from cyber threats and data breaches.
  • Arduino & Raspberry Pi: Popular development platforms for building IoT applications and prototypes.
