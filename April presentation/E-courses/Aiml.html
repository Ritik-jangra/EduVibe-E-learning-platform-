What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think, learn, and make decisions like humans.

Types of AI

  • Narrow AI (Weak AI): AI designed for a specific task. E.g., voice assistants like Alexa and Siri.
  • General AI (Strong AI): AI that can understand and learn any intellectual task like a human being.
  • Super AI: Hypothetical AI that surpasses human intelligence in all aspects.

What is Machine Learning?

Machine Learning (ML) is a subset of AI that allows systems to learn from data and improve performance without being explicitly programmed.

Types of Machine Learning

  • Supervised Learning: Uses labeled datasets to train algorithms that classify data or predict outcomes.
  • Unsupervised Learning: Uses data without labels to find hidden patterns or data groupings.
  • Semi-Supervised Learning: Uses a small amount of labeled data with a large amount of unlabeled data to train models.
  • Reinforcement Learning: Trains models to make sequences of decisions through trial and error and feedback.

Common Algorithms in ML

  • Linear Regression
  • Logistic Regression
  • Decision Trees
  • Random Forest
  • K-Means Clustering
  • Naive Bayes
  • Support Vector Machines (SVM)
  • Neural Networks

More Concepts in AIML

  • Neural Networks: A series of algorithms modeled after the human brain that are designed to recognize patterns and interpret sensory data.
  • Deep Learning: A specialized form of neural networks with multiple layers (deep architectures) that can learn complex patterns in large amounts of data.
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP): A field of AI that enables machines to understand, interpret, and respond to human language (text or speech).
  • Computer Vision: A field of AI that trains computers to interpret and understand visual information from the world, such as images and videos.

Applications of AI & ML

  • Healthcare (Diagnosis, Predictive Analysis)
  • Finance (Fraud Detection, Stock Prediction)
  • Retail (Recommendation Systems)
  • Autonomous Vehicles
  • Chatbots & Virtual Assistants
  • Smart Cities
