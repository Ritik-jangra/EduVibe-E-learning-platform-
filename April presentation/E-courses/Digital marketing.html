What is Digital Marketing?

Digital Marketing is the use of online platforms and technologies to promote products and services through digital channels such as search engines, websites, social media, email, and mobile apps.

What is SEO?

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the process of optimizing website content to rank higher on search engine results and increase organic traffic.

Key Concepts

  • SEO Strategies: Keyword research, backlink building, content optimization, and technical SEO.
  • Social Media Marketing: Promoting content and engagement through platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn.
