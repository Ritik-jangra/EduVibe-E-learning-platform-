Frequently Asked Questions

What is EduVibe?

EduVibe is an e-learning platform that offers a variety of courses to help students enhance their skills.

How do I enroll in a course?

You can enroll by signing up, selecting a course, and making a payment (if applicable).

Are the courses free?

EduVibe offers both free and paid courses. The details are mentioned in each course description.

Can I get a certificate after course completion?

Yes! After successfully completing a course, you will receive a certificate of completion.

What types of courses are available?

EduVibe offers courses in various fields like programming, data science, business, marketing, and more.

Is there a refund policy?

Yes, we offer a refund within 7 days of enrollment for eligible courses.

Do instructors provide support?

Yes, instructors and the EduVibe support team are available to answer your questions.

Can I download course materials?

Some courses provide downloadable materials, while others may require online access.

How long do I have access to the courses?

Most courses offer lifetime access, but some may have limited access periods.
